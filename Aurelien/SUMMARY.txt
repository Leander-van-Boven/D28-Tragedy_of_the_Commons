The Tragedy of the Commons in Evolutionary Biology - 2007
	- categorises different forms of TotC.
	- 2 "social goods": Cooperative + uncompetetive.
	- a "component tragedy": a low amount of resources, but the population is just surviving.
	- Forms of solving the tragedy: restraining individuals, divide population, cooperation + punishment, diminishing returns + priorities.
	- What is the frequency of a TotC arising over time in a population?
